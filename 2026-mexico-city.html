Bike ride on Reforma, explore the historic center

Morning

Afternoon

  • Lunch at Cafe de Tacuba in Centro Historico
  • Zocalo & Catedral Metropolitana
  • Palacio Nacional (Diego Rivera murals)
  • Templo Mayor ruins and museum
  • Walk down Calle Madero to Palacio de Bellas Artes

Evening

  • Museo Nacional de Antropologia (open until 5pm)
  • Dinner and mezcal tasting in Roma Norte
