Key Intuitions

Android: Fragmentation is the Feature

Android's power is choice — OEMs customize everything. The tradeoff: only ~19% of devices run the latest version. Google now ships 2 major releases/year to close the gap.

iOS: Uniformity is the Feature

Apple controls hardware + software. Result: ~80% adoption of latest iOS within months. The jump from iOS 18→26 (skipping 19-25) unifies version numbering across all Apple platforms.

Convergence

Both OSes have converged on the same core features: widgets, privacy dashboards, AI assistants, always-on displays, customizable lock screens, and on-device ML. The real differentiator is now ecosystem, not features.

Android Update Revolution

Project Treble (Android 8+) separated vendor code from OS. Project Mainline (Android 10+) lets Google update core modules via Play Store — bypassing OEMs entirely. This fundamentally changed the update story.

iOS Support Window

Apple now supports iPhones for 6-7 years with major OS updates. The iPhone 11 (2019) still runs iOS 26 in 2025. No Android OEM matches this except Google's 7-year Pixel promise.

The AI Pivot (2024–2026)

Both platforms bet big on on-device AI: Apple Intelligence (iOS 18/26) and Gemini Nano (Android 14+). This is the defining battleground going forward — who owns the intelligent layer between you and your apps.
