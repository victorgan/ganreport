# Mexico City Trip: March 7-16, 2026

## Day 1 - Saturday, March 7: Arrival
- Fly into Mexico City (MEX)
- Check into hotel in Roma Norte
- Evening walk around the neighborhood
- Dinner at a local taqueria

## Day 2 - Sunday, March 8: Centro Historico
- Zocalo (main square) and Catedral Metropolitana
- Palacio Nacional (Diego Rivera murals)
- Templo Mayor ruins and museum
- Lunch at Cafe de Tacuba
- Walk down Calle Madero to Palacio de Bellas Artes
- Evening mezcal tasting in Roma Norte

## Day 3 - Monday, March 9: Chapultepec
- Chapultepec Castle and views of the city
- Museo Nacional de Antropologia (spend the afternoon)
- Walk through Bosque de Chapultepec
- Dinner in Polanco

## Day 4 - Tuesday, March 10: Coyoacan & Xochimilco
- Morning at Frida Kahlo Museum (Casa Azul) - book tickets in advance
- Explore Coyoacan market and town square
- Afternoon trajinera boat ride on Xochimilco canals
- Dinner in Coyoacan

## Day 5 - Wednesday, March 11: Condesa & Roma
- Morning at Mercado Roma for brunch
- Walk Avenida Amsterdam loop in Condesa
- Visit Museo Tamayo or MUAC
- Afternoon coffee and bookshops in Roma
- Dinner at Contramar (reserve ahead)

## Day 6 - Thursday, March 12: Day Trip to Teotihuacan
- Early departure to Teotihuacan pyramids
- Climb the Pyramid of the Sun and Pyramid of the Moon
- Walk the Avenue of the Dead
- Lunch near the site
- Return to the city in the afternoon
- Relaxed evening, rooftop drinks

## Day 7 - Friday, March 13: Markets & Food Tour
- Morning at Mercado de la Merced (largest market in the city)
- Street food tour: tamales, tlacoyos, quesadillas, elote
- Afternoon at Mercado de San Juan (gourmet ingredients)
- Visit Biblioteca Vasconcelos
- Dinner at Pujol or Quintonil (reserve well in advance)

## Day 8 - Saturday, March 14: Art & Culture
- Morning at UNAM campus (murals and architecture)
- Museo Universitario Arte Contemporaneo (MUAC)
- Afternoon at Museo Soumaya in Polanco (free entry)
- Walk Avenida Presidente Masaryk for shopping
- Evening lucha libre at Arena Mexico

## Day 9 - Sunday, March 15: San Angel & Leisure
- Sunday art bazaar at Plaza San Jacinto in San Angel
- Brunch at San Angel Inn
- Afternoon at Museo de El Carmen
- Pack and relax at hotel
- Farewell dinner in Roma Norte

## Day 10 - Monday, March 16: Departure
- Morning breakfast at a local cafe
- Last-minute souvenir shopping
- Depart from MEX

---

## Notes
- **Accommodation:** Roma Norte or Condesa recommended
- **Transport:** Metro + Uber for getting around; arrange private transport for Teotihuacan
- **Reservations needed:** Frida Kahlo Museum, Contramar, Pujol/Quintonil
- **Currency:** Mexican Peso (MXN)
- **Weather:** Warm and dry, highs around 25C / 77F
