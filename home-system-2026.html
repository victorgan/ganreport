1. Site & Ground Systems

Managing water and soil gases before they touch the structure.

A. Grading & Water Management

The Problem: Hydrostatic pressure forces water through concrete; poor grading floods basements.

SOTA

Exterior Weeping Tile + Dimple Mat

Full plastic dimple membrane wraps the foundation wall, creating an air gap so water falls instantly to the drain pipe. Swales divert surface water 10ft+ away.

VALUE

Positive Grading + Damp Proofing

Ensure soil slopes away from house (5% grade) and spray a bitumen coating on concrete walls.

RENTAL

Gutter Extensions

Buy flexible plastic tubes to attach to downspouts, pushing roof water 6ft away from the wall.

Critical Detail: Do not use mulch against the foundation (it holds water). Use 12 inches of gravel (a "sterile zone").

B. Radon Mitigation

The Problem: Radioactive soil gas (lung cancer risk) enters airtight homes.

SOTA

Active Sub-Slab Depressurization

Fan constantly sucks air from gravel layer under basement slab and vents above roof.

VALUE

Passive Rough-In

Install pipe through slab/roof but cap it. Add fan ($300) only if test shows high levels.

RENTAL

Airtight Seal + Dilution

Seal basement cracks with caulk. If levels high, crack a window to dilute gas.

2. Structure & Sheathing

The Shell: Resistance to Loads, Wind, and Thermal Bridging.

A. Foundation

The Problem: Load transfer and thermal loss into the earth.

SOTA

ICF (Insulated Concrete Forms)

Foam blocks stacked and filled with concrete. R-22+ performance insulated inside and out.

VALUE

Poured Concrete + Interior Blanket

Standard poured walls with interior framed wall insulated with batt.

N/A

No Option

Check for musty smells which indicate foundation leaks.

B. Framing & Sheathing

The Problem: Structural rigidity and air/water sealing.

SOTA

Engineered Lumber + ZIP R-Sheathing

Straight engineered studs (LSL/LVL). ZIP R-Sheathing has integrated foam and air barrier, eliminating house wrap.

VALUE

2x6 Stick Framing + OSB + Tyvek

The standard code. 2x6 allows more insulation than old 2x4 walls.

N/A

Selection

Select concrete buildings for sound isolation.

3. Building Envelope

The Control Layers: Heat, Air, and Moisture.

A. Insulation

The Problem: Heat flow (cost) and condensation.

SOTA

Exterior Continuous + Spray Foam

Wrapping outside keeps studs warm. Spray foam seals all gaps.

VALUE

Flash-and-Batt

1 inch spray foam for air sealing, followed by fiberglass or mineral wool.

RENTAL

Window Film

"Shrink wrap" plastic kits create trapped air pocket for glass insulation.

B. Windows

The Problem: The "hole" in the wall. Thermal loss and drafts.

SOTA

Triple-Pane Tilt-and-Turn

R-7 value (European style). Multi-point locking mechanism seals like a bank vault.

VALUE

Double-Pane Low-E

Argon filled with "Warm Edge" rubber spacers (not metal).

RENTAL

Thermal Curtains + Bubble Wrap

Heavy velvet curtains stop drafts. Reflective bubble foil for heatwaves.

C. Flashing (Waterproofing)

The Problem: Leaks at joints/seams.

SOTA

Liquid Applied Flashing

Thick rubbery paste painted on sills. Forms a seamless, monolithic boot.

VALUE

Acrylic Flashing Tape

ZIP Tape or 3M. Superior to asphalt; sticks to wet surfaces and doesn't dry out.

N/A

Visual Inspection

Look for water stains at top corners of windows.

4. MEP Systems

Life Support: Air, Water, and Power.

A. HVAC (Heating & Cooling)

The Problem: Comfort and efficiency.

SOTA

Cold Climate Heat Pump

Air-to-Air (Mitsubishi/Fujitsu). Works down to -30Â°C. Individual zoning. No gas required.

VALUE

Central Heat Pump + Electric Backup

Ducted system. Electric resistance strips kick in for the coldest 5 days of the year.

RENTAL

Portable Dual-Hose AC + Vornado

Dual-hose prevents negative pressure. Vornado fan mixes air to break hot/cold spots.

B. Ventilation (IAQ)

The Problem: Stale air, CO2, and chemicals in airtight spaces.

SOTA

ERV - Dedicated Ducting

Extracts from wet rooms, supplies bedrooms. Recovers 80% heat & moisture.

VALUE

HRV (Heat Recovery Ventilator)

Transfers heat only (not moisture). May need humidifier in winter.

RENTAL

CO2 Monitor + HEPA

Watch Aranet4 monitor; if >1000ppm, crack window. Run HEPA for dust.

C. Water Heating

The Problem: High energy use (2nd highest).

SOTA

Hybrid Heat Pump Heater

Steals heat from basement air. Dehumidifies as side effect. 300% efficiency.

VALUE

Standard Electric Tank + Timer

Simple, durable. Timer restricts heating to off-peak rate hours.

N/A

Recirc Test

Run tap. If hot water takes >30s, recirc line is missing.

D. Electrical & Data

The Problem: Powering the digital/electric future.

SOTA

400 Amp + Solar Ready + Cat6a

Conduit to roof. PoE Ethernet to every room and camera location.

VALUE

200 Amp + Smurf Tube

Blue flexible conduit from panel to attic for future wire pulling without cutting walls.

RENTAL

UPS (Battery Backup)

Battery backup for router/PC to maintain internet during micro-outages.

5. Finishes (Skin)

Durability and Maintenance.

Flooring & Siding

The Problem: Wear, tear, moisture, and curb appeal.

SOTA

Engineered Hardwood + Yakisugi

Thick wear layer wood (stable). Charred Cedar siding (rot-proof, no painting for 50yrs).

VALUE

LVP + Fiber Cement

Stone Polymer Composite flooring (waterproof). Hardie Board siding (fire resistant, paint every 15yrs).

RENTAL

Area Rugs + Thick Underpad

Covers ugly floors and provides critical acoustic dampening.

Drywall & Interior

The Problem: Fire safety and sound transmission.

SOTA

5/8" Type X Drywall

Used everywhere. Thicker, better soundproofing, straighter walls, higher fire rating.

VALUE

1/2" Light Drywall + Solid Doors

Save on walls, splurge on solid core doors to block noise.

RENTAL

Acoustic Panels/Art

Canvas art with foam backing to reduce echo.

6. Micro-Stressor Management

Physiological Health Hacking.

A. Water Quality

The Problem: Chlorine, heavy metals, hardness.

SOTA

Whole Home Carbon + UV + Softener

Bottled quality from every tap. UV kills bacteria.

VALUE

Under-Sink RO

Reverse Osmosis for perfect drinking water at kitchen tap only.

RENTAL

Shower Filter + Countertop RO

Reduces skin irritation and microplastics (e.g. AquaTru + Sprite).

B. Lighting

The Problem: Circadian disruption (blue light at night).

SOTA

Lutron Ketra

Automatically matches color temp of sun throughout day (Human Centric).

VALUE

Dim-to-Warm LEDs

Bulbs get warmer (orange) as dimmed, mimicking sunset.

RENTAL

Smart Bulbs (Hue)

Program to turn red/orange automatically at 8:00 PM.

C. Acoustics

The Problem: Noise pollution (Cortisol spike).

SOTA

Resilient Channel + QuietRock

Drywall decoupled from studs via metal clips to absorb vibration.

VALUE

Safe-n-Sound Insulation

Stuff interior walls with mineral wool batts instead of leaving hollow.

RENTAL

Mechanical White Noise

Physical fan (Dohm) creates sound blanket masking elevator/hallway noise.
