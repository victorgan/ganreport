Odin 3 Play Modes

Three ways to use it

The Classic handles 2D retro. The Odin 3 covers everything else across three play modes.

Mode 1 — Emulation
N64 through Wii
N64, Dreamcast, PSP, PS2, GameCube, Wii, DS. Dolphin and AetherSX2 widescreen hacks take advantage of the 16:9 panel.
Mode 2 — Native Android
PC indie ports
Into the Breach, Slay the Spire, Celeste, Hades, Balatro, FTL all have Android versions. No latency, works offline.
Mode 3 — PC Streaming
Sunshine + Moonlight
Stream your PC library over WiFi. Touchscreen acts as mouse. Turn-based and point-and-click genres work well since latency doesn't matter much.

03 — PC Streaming

Moonlight streaming picks

Sunshine on PC, Moonlight on Odin 3. Turn-based and mouse-driven games stream well because 30ms of latency doesn't affect gameplay.

Setup tip
Make a dedicated “handheld” shelf in Steam or a Playnite collection so you're not scrolling your full library on a 6″ screen.
Dual-option games
Slay the Spire, Into the Breach, and Balatro have both native Android and PC versions. Use native for travel, stream at home if you want save sync.
