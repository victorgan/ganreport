Tour de Washington

21 stages over 21 days. Two parallel tours — a TdF-matching full version and a weekday-doable SLU-local version. Every stage has both.

Overview

Full version: ~2,600 km · ~28,500 m of climbing · 7 weekend-required stages · 4 overnight trips.

Weekday version: ~1,500 km · ~15,000 m · all stages from SLU or Fremont in 1.5–3 hours. Mountain days become local simulators.

Structure: Week 1 (Stages 1–7) Settling In · Rest Day · Week 2 (8–15) Mountains Awaken · Rest Day · Week 3 (16–21) Queen Week.

Maps are approximate bounded views of each stage area — they show geography, not a route line. Build accurate GPX in RideWithGPS before riding.

Week 1 — Settling In

Seattle metro. Ferry-accessible. SLU-start-doable. Build form without blowing up.

1. Queen Anne Prologue — ITT

Short, steep urban ITT. The Counterbalance shatters the field before breakfast.

Full · 14 km / 350 m  ·  Weekday · 7 km / 175 m
Start · SLU  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★☆☆☆

Full: 2 laps. Counterbalance (Queen Anne Ave N, 0.8 km at 10–15%) → Highland Dr → Kerry Park → descend Taylor Ave → Mercer → repeat.

Weekday: Same route, one lap. 25-minute all-out opener, pre-work.

TdF parallel: Düsseldorf Prologue 2017  ·  Iconic: Kerry Park skyline at the top of each lap

2. Snohomish Valley — Sprint

Long, flat, fast. Wind-dependent — easterlies off the Cascades can turn benign into brutal.

Full · 180 km / 1,000 m  ·  Weekday · 65 km / 300 m
Start · SLU  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★☆☆

Full: SLU → Burke-Gilman Trail → Kenmore → Centennial Trail → Arlington → Stanwood → return via Snohomish Valley roads → Woodinville → BGT → SLU.

Weekday: SLU → BGT → Sammamish River Trail → Marymoor → return.

TdF parallel: Bayonne-style Week 1 bunch sprint  ·  Iconic: Stanwood flats with Mt Baker on the horizon

3. Seattle 7 Hills+ — Punchy

Repeated short-steep climbs. Hills don't end — they change name.

Full · 140 km / 2,200 m  ·  Weekday · 65 km / 1,200 m
Start · SLU  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★☆

Full: Queen Anne (Counterbalance) → Magnolia Blvd → Phinney Ridge → Capitol Hill (E Olive Way) → First Hill (James St) → Beacon Hill (S Spokane) → West Seattle (Admiral + Avalon). Extensions: Mercer Island loop, Juanita Hill.

Weekday: Core 7 Hills only, west-of-I-5. 2.5 hours evening.

TdF parallel: Mur de Bretagne finish  ·  Iconic: Magnolia Blvd with Olympic Mountains across the Sound

4. Snoqualmie Valley Farms — Flat+

Protected trails, farm roads, one climb out at the end to remind you this isn't Holland.

Full · 150 km / 800 m  ·  Weekday · 70 km / 500 m
Start · SLU  ·  Bike · Road or gravel-light  ·  Difficulty · ★★★☆☆

Full: SLU → BGT → SRT → Redmond → Snoqualmie Valley Trail → Duvall → Carnation → Snoqualmie → Fall City → climb out via Issaquah-Fall City Rd → I-90 trail → SLU.

Weekday: Short version — SLU → BGT → SRT → Snoqualmie Valley Trail from Redmond → return.

TdF parallel: Breakaway day (Saint-Amand)  ·  Iconic: Carnation farmland at golden hour

5. Bainbridge Inner Loop — Medium Mountain

Rolling island with no flat. The legs never quite get to rest.

Full · 100 km / 1,200 m  ·  Weekday · 80 km / 1,000 m
Start · SLU → Colman Dock ferry  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★☆☆

Full: SLU → Elliott Bay → Colman Dock → ferry → Winslow. Full Chilly Hilly-style loop: Battle Point → Fletcher Bay → Lynwood Center → Point Monroe → Rolling Bay → Kitsap Memorial → return.

Weekday: Catch 5:30 or 6:10am ferry. Full loop. 9am return ferry for remote-work start.

TdF parallel: Corsica 2013 rolling  ·  Iconic: Agate Pass Bridge at sunrise on the early ferry

6. Issaquah Alps — Hilly

Four short-sharp summits in one day. The closest thing WA has to an Ardennes classic.

Full · 120 km / 2,800 m  ·  Weekday · 65 km / 1,500 m
Start · SLU  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★☆

Full: SLU → I-90 Trail → Issaquah → Zoo Hill (Tiger Mtn Rd east) → Tiger summit → Squak (Mountainside Dr) → Cougar (Lakemont Blvd) → Newcastle → Coal Creek → I-90 home.

Weekday: I-90 trail → Cougar via Lakemont + Zoo Hill → back. 3 hours.

TdF parallel: Flèche Wallonne / Liège-Bastogne-Liège  ·  Iconic: Tiger Mtn east summit looking south to Rainier

7. Green River + Interurban — Sprint

Industrial flat. Different aesthetic from Snohomish — same sprint training.

Full · 130 km / 400 m  ·  Weekday · 55 km / 200 m
Start · SLU  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★☆☆☆

Full: SLU → waterfront → West Seattle Bridge → Duwamish Trail → Green River Trail → Kent → Auburn → Interurban Trail → Tukwila → return via Beacon Hill or Rainier Valley.

Weekday: Interurban Trail south from Beacon Hill to Kent and back.

TdF parallel: Transitional sprint stage  ·  Iconic: Boeing plant with Rainier framed through power lines

Rest Day I
Easy Lake Union spin. Brunch. Bike shop run — drivetrain clean, tire swap for mountain week. Verify pass conditions.

Week 2 — Mountains Awaken

First Cascade assaults. Overnights start here. One flat ITT bisects the mountain block.

8. Snoqualmie Pass — High Mountain ▲ Weekend

First real pass. Moderate grade, long approach, tests the legs before the bigger ones.

Full · 120 km / 1,200 m  ·  Weekday sim · 50 km / 1,500 m
Start · Issaquah (30min drive)  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★☆

Full: Issaquah → Issaquah-Fall City Rd → Fall City → Snoqualmie → North Bend → Edgewick → I-90 shoulder (or Palouse to Cascades Trail) → Snoqualmie Pass summit (3,022 ft) → descend → loop back via Snoqualmie Valley.

Weekday simulator: Zoo Hill × 4 repeats = ~1,500m vert. Captures sustained-climb workload in 3 hours from SLU.

TdF parallel: Alpe du Grand Serre · mid-mountain introduction  ·  Iconic: Franklin Falls turnout on the descent

9. Mt Baker Hwy → Artist Point — Summit Finish ▲ Weekend

Iconic summit finish. Twenty-four miles of climb, steep third pitch, glaciers at the top.

Full · 170 km / 1,400 m (from Bellingham)  ·  Weekday sim · 60 km / 1,100 m
Start · Bellingham (overnight)  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★★

Full: Bellingham → SR-542 → Deming → Kendall → Maple Falls → Glacier → climb to Artist Point (5,140 ft). From Glacier: ~40 km at 4–5% steady with a steep final 2 miles. Descend same.

Weekday simulator: Cougar Mtn via Lakemont Blvd × 3. Sustained climb workload, ~1,100m vert.

TdF parallel: Plateau de Beille · paved summit finish  ·  Iconic: Artist Point with Mt Shuksan reflected in snowmelt pools

10. Olympic Discovery Trail — Flat Transition

Recovery-paced transition through the rain shadow. Easy day. You need it.

Full · 110 km / 400 m  ·  Weekday · 80 km / 400 m
Start · Edmonds → Kingston ferry  ·  Bike · Road or gravel-light  ·  Difficulty · ★★☆☆☆

Full: Edmonds → ferry → Kingston → shuttle or ride to Port Townsend → Olympic Discovery Trail east (Port Townsend → Discovery Bay → Sequim → Port Angeles).

Weekday: Edmonds-Kingston ferry → Hansville loop or out-and-back.

TdF parallel: Bordeaux transition recovery flat  ·  Iconic: Sequim lavender fields in full July bloom

11. Chuckanut + Skagit — Hilly

Cliff-hugging coastal road. Tourists stop at every pullout — ride early or ride late.

Full · 140 km / 1,500 m  ·  Weekday · 45 km / 1,000 m
Start · Bellingham (Amtrak or drive)  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★☆☆

Full: Bellingham → Chuckanut Drive south → Edison → Bow → Samish Island loop → return via Skagit Valley flats → Blanchard → Chuckanut north → Bellingham.

Weekday: Juanita Hill × 6 or Cougar Mtn north face. Captures coastal-punchy character locally.

TdF parallel: Brittany coastal stages (Lorient, Saint-Malo)  ·  Iconic: Chuckanut cliffs over Samish Bay at low tide

12. Vashon + Maury — Medium Mountain

Relentlessly hilly island. Every turn is a kicker. Nothing long, nothing easy.

Full · 100 km / 1,600 m  ·  Weekday · 55 km / 800 m
Start · Fauntleroy ferry  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★☆

Full: SLU → West Seattle → Fauntleroy → ferry → Vashon perimeter + Maury (Point Robinson) → Tahlequah → Point Defiance ferry → Tacoma → Sounder train home.

Weekday: Early Fauntleroy ferry → Vashon north-end loop → return.

TdF parallel: Corsica II · short relentless rolling  ·  Iconic: Point Robinson Lighthouse with Mt Rainier behind

13. Mercer Island ITT — Flat ITT

The flat ITT. Pure power. East Mercer Way adds teeth.

Full · 40 km / 250 m  ·  Weekday · same
Start · SLU  ·  Bike · TT / Aero road  ·  Difficulty · ★★☆☆☆

Full: SLU → I-90 trail → Mercer Island → perimeter via East Mercer Way (quieter, hillier) + West Mercer Way → return via I-90 trail → SLU.

Weekday: Same route. Pre-work or evening TT effort. Already commute-compatible.

TdF parallel: Utrecht ITT 2015 · flat, rolling, clean  ·  Iconic: Lake Washington from the East Mercer Way bluffs

14. N Cascades Hwy — Queen Mountain ▲ Weekend

The queen stage. High, remote, scenic, unforgiving. The Col du Tourmalet of Washington.

Full · 160 km / 2,800 m  ·  Weekday sim · 90 km / 2,000 m
Start · Winthrop / Mazama (overnight)  ·  Bike · Road, climbing gearing  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★★

Full: Mazama → SR-20 west → Washington Pass (5,477 ft) → Rainy Pass (4,855 ft) → descend to Diablo Lake → climb back over both → return to Winthrop.

Weekday simulator: Saturday ride of all four Issaquah Alps summits — 2,000m vert in one SLU-start day.

TdF parallel: Col du Tourmalet day  ·  Iconic: Washington Pass overlook with Liberty Bell spires

15. Stevens Pass via US-2 — High Mountain ▲ Weekend

Long sustained climb up the Skykomish valley. Less brutal than Washington Pass; more grinding.

Full · 170 km / 1,700 m  ·  Weekday sim · 70 km / 1,700 m
Start · Sultan (drive)  ·  Bike · Road, climbing gearing  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★☆

Full: Sultan → Gold Bar → Index → Skykomish → Stevens Pass summit (4,061 ft) → out-and-back. Extended: descend to Leavenworth, loop back via Blewett Pass or Amtrak.

Weekday simulator: Cougar Mtn north face × 4 repeats. 4 × 25 min of sustained climbing.

TdF parallel: Col du Galibier · sustained climb  ·  Iconic: Stevens Pass summit with ski lifts skeletal in July

Rest Day II
Recovery ride or full off-bike depending on fatigue. Travel day for Hurricane Ridge overnight. Chain inspection after two weeks.

Week 3 — Queen Week

Final assaults, mountain ITT, RAMROD finale. The hardest week by any measure.

16. Whidbey Island — Hilly

Rolling coastal north of Seattle. Deception Pass is the iconic moment; the miles before earn it.

Full · 120 km / 1,400 m  ·  Weekday · 70 km / 800 m
Start · Mukilteo ferry  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★☆☆

Full: Mukilteo → ferry → Clinton → Whidbey full length (Langley, Coupeville, Oak Harbor) → Deception Pass → Anacortes → return via Amtrak (Mt Vernon) or reverse ride.

Weekday: Edmonds-Kingston ferry + Kitsap hill loop. 70 km / 800 m.

TdF parallel: Brittany rolling coastal II  ·  Iconic: Deception Pass Bridge from the north approach

17. Kitsap + Hood Canal — Medium Mountain

Quiet peninsula. Remote, rolling, the kind of day where breakaways go unnoticed.

Full · 120 km / 1,600 m  ·  Weekday · 60 km / 700 m
Start · Bainbridge ferry  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★☆☆

Full: SLU → Colman → ferry → Winslow → north on SR-305 → Poulsbo → Hood Canal Bridge → south on SR-3 → Seabeck → Silverdale → Bremerton → ferry home.

Weekday: Short Bainbridge loop. 60 km / 700 m.

TdF parallel: Jura quiet stages  ·  Iconic: Seabeck Harbor with Olympics reflected on a still morning

18. Hurricane Ridge — Summit Finish ▲ Weekend

The Ventoux of the Olympics. Seventeen miles up, no breaks, exposed.

Full · 100 km / 1,700 m  ·  Weekday sim · 50 km / 1,600 m
Start · Port Angeles (overnight)  ·  Bike · Road, climbing gearing  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★★

Full: Port Angeles → Hurricane Ridge Rd → summit (5,242 ft) → descend. 27 km / 1,580m at sustained 5–7%. Extended: add Lake Crescent coastal loop.

Weekday simulator: Zoo Hill × 4. Captures sustained climbing at sustained effort.

TdF parallel: Mont Ventoux  ·  Iconic: Hurricane Ridge visitor center with Olympic interior glaciated peaks

19. Centennial Trail Full — Flat

Pre-queen transition. Spin the legs. Don't tax them.

Full · 180 km / 600 m  ·  Weekday · 90 km / 400 m
Start · SLU  ·  Bike · Road  ·  Difficulty · ★★★☆☆

Full: SLU → BGT → Kenmore → Centennial Trail full length (Snohomish → Nakashima Heritage Barn, ~48 km one way) → return. Optional Skagit extension via Silvana.

Weekday: SLU → BGT → Snohomish → short Centennial segment → return.

TdF parallel: Pre-queen transition flat  ·  Iconic: Nakashima Heritage Barn on the Arlington extension

20. Sunrise Rd ITT — Mountain ITT ▲ Weekend

Uphill TT. Steady grade, sustained power. Pure suffering at altitude.

Full · 50 km / 1,100 m  ·  Weekday sim · 30 km / 800 m
Start · SR-410 Sunrise Rd turnoff  ·  Bike · Road, light wheels  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★☆

Full: Drive to SR-410 Sunrise Rd turnoff. Pure uphill TT: 26 km / 1,036 m at steady 5–6% to Sunrise Point (6,400 ft). Descend same for 50 km total.

Weekday simulator: Zoo Hill TT × 3 all-out attempts. Same power demand, different altitude.

TdF parallel: La Planche des Belles Filles ITT · decisive uphill TT  ·  Iconic: Sunrise Point with Rainier's east face dominating

21. RAMROD — Queen Finale ▲ Weekend

Ride Around Mount Rainier in One Day. 250 km. 3,000 m. Five passes. The final exam.

Full · 250 km / 3,050 m  ·  Weekday sim · 120 km / 2,500 m
Start · Enumclaw (overnight)  ·  Bike · Road, full climbing kit  ·  Difficulty · ★★★★★

Full: Enumclaw → SR-410 → Cayuse Pass (4,694 ft) → Chinook Pass (5,430 ft) out-and-back → SR-123 south → Ohanapecosh → Stevens Canyon Rd → Paradise (5,400 ft) → SR-706 → Longmire → Elbe → Eatonville → Enumclaw.

Weekday simulator: Saturday local ultimate — 7 Hills + Issaquah Alps full loop. 2,500m of local vert. Captures total workload of the finale.

TdF parallel: Stage 20 queen + ceremonial merged  ·  Iconic: Paradise Inn with Mt Rainier's south face in morning light

Cross-Stage Risks

  • Pass opening dates. SR-20, Sunrise Rd, and Paradise can open late May through early July. Verify WSDOT weekly in June before booking.
  • Ferry delays. Stages 5, 10, 12, 16, 17. Carry a 90-minute buffer for return ferry.
  • Wildfire smoke. Stage 14 most at risk. Check AQI the morning of; have indoor fallback.
  • Heat. Stages 14, 15, 21 can hit 35°C. Pre-dawn starts; carry 2L minimum.
  • Traffic on US-2. Stage 15. Logging trucks, narrow shoulder. Front/rear lights always.
  • Mechanical on RAMROD. 250 km from services. Two tubes, spare tire, chain quick-link, 3L water capacity.
  • Belgium collision. Ardennes tour July 12–15 will overlap 3–4 TdW stages. Decide in April whether to pre-ride or shift the whole TdW by a week.
